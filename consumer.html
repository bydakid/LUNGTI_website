COMING SOON

Annually, a staggering 2.5 billion tons of food go to waste.

We envision a world free of food waste. Imagine the vast quantities of unsold food from your beloved restaurants, supermarkets, cafes, and shops that go to waste each day - a casualty of not being sold in time. Our Lungti Food app streamlines the process of making a difference. It offers you the chance to rescue 'Mystery Bags' of quality food at attractive prices. Together, we can curtail food waste and enjoy great meals.

How it works

01
Search

Explore the app and discover nearby 'Mystery Bags' of food from local eateries and stores.
02
Choose

Select your desired 'Mystery Bag' from the app, claim it, and finalize your purchase.
03
Collect

Then, journey to the designated store at the allotted time to uncover your surprise.
